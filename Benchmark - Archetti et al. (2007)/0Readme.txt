The instances and the following description can also be found at: https://www.leandro-coelho.com/instances/inventory-routing/

For these instances the following holds:

First row: number of retailers, number of considered days, transportation capacity
Second row: Depot, x-coordinate, y-coordinate, initial inventory, daily replenishment, holding costs
Remaining rows: Customer, x-coordinate, y-coordinate, initial inventory, Order-up-to-level, a value that is always zero and irrelevant for us, daily demand, holding costs

Note that the distances between customers is the euclidean distance that is rounded to the nearest integer number
